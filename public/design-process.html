Design Process

Architecture is the experience of form, time and function. Most often we are concerned only with functional necessity, e.g., shelter and work. That's like painting a rainbow with only two colors. Architecture has the ability to elicit emotions of strength, connection, playfulness, purpose, and beauty, drawing on the full spectrum of possibilities and experiences. On your journey, will you invest in the beauty, strength, playfulness, and connection provided by the built environment?
